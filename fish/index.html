Do Fish Drink
Water? 🐟

Some fish gulp water all day. Others never drink — they just pee a lot! Let's find out why.

👋 For grown-ups
This game teaches osmosis in kid words. Sit together and tap 🔊 Hear it so a pre-reader can play. The big idea: a fish is like a water balloon with tiny holes. Salty ocean water pulls water OUT of the fish, so saltwater fish must drink to refill (and push salt out their gills). Fresh water pushes IN, so freshwater fish get too full and make lots of pee. About 4 minutes.
